How to Play

Controls

  • WASD to move
  • Mouse to aim and click to attack
  • SPACEBAR for Ultimate
  • Shift+1 / Shift+2 / Shift+3 for Emotes
  • Enter to Chat

Objective

Destroy the enemy base, eat orbs to level up, and upgrade stats!

The Roster

  • Warrior (150 HP): AoE Stun
  • Archer (80 HP): Piercing Laser
  • Mage (90 HP): Black Hole Pull
  • Priest (120 HP): AoE Burst Heal
  • Assassin (70 HP): Teleport to Enemy
  • Summoner (100 HP): Spawn AI Minions
  • Chaos Witch (90 HP): Reverse Enemy Controls
  • Engineer (120 HP): Deploy Auto-Turret
